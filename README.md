## What Is SerfStack?

SerfStack is a server hosting provider that rents out VPS/VDS, dedicated servers, and GPU servers. They have data centers in Russia (certified TIER-III facilities in Moscow and St. Petersburg), the USA (California and Florida), Europe (Germany, Finland), and the Netherlands.

The company is registered as an LLC in California (File Number 202359610208), so it operates properly as a US-registered business despite having its roots in Russia. That matters if you're concerned about company legitimacy.

Their bread and butter is straightforward: you need a server, they have one, and they rent it to you at a price that starts from "a few cups of coffee" per month — their words, not mine, but honestly not wrong.

## Who Is SerfStack For?

Honestly? It fits a few different types pretty well:

- **Developers and small teams** who need a reliable VPS in Europe without paying European premium prices
